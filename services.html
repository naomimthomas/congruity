About Us

Congruity Advisors is a customer engagement advisory firm who works with clients to drive customer-oriented business transformation. Whether our clients are interested in customer intimacy transformation initiatives that move from product-centric to solution-oriented business models, or engineering truly meaningful customer journeys that differentiate them in the today’s marketplace, Congruity Advisors helps companies drive successful outcomes. We embed practices within their culture and operating processes that will ignite the customer experience and drive differentiated brand recognition to thrive in a volatile business climate.

Guided by our client’s business priorities, we help our clients define a customer engagement vision that is aligned, integrated and culturally embraced across the enterprise. We explore business and operational models to develop new ways to optimize and enrich the customer engagement process, and drive customer loyalty by engaging with customers, employees, and partners in thoughtful, authentic and repeatable ways. Once developed, this customer engagement vision serves as the framework for how new technologies and innovation can be leveraged to support our client’s customer experience.

Our Four Core Tenets

We founded Congruity Advisors based on the following four core tenants:

Customer Experience Drives
Brand Differentiation

Thoughtful and successful engagement with customers satisfies emotional perceptions and expectations that in turn generate loyalty and competitive differentiation.

Customer Experience Improvement Requires
A Holistic Approach

The transformation to a differentiated, customer-centric enterprise is not a matter of changing one big idea, but rather changing a lot of little ideas, and tightly integrating them to create a holistic, enterprise-wide approach.

Customer Experience Improvement Is
A Transformation, Not A Project

Building the capabilities to consistently delight customers doesn’t happen overnight. The process to get there is a transformation journey that requires a corporate commitment over multi-year timeframe.

Customer Experience Journey
Is Worth The Effort

Transformation efforts aren’t easy, but leading your company to be more customer-centric is worth the effort. We are committed to helping our clients accelerate their results and avoid many of the obstacles along the way.
