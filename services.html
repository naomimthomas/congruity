Strategy & Operations

Executable strategy requires fusing insightful thinking with disciplined execution to achieve breakthrough performance.  Today’s senior executives grapple with attaining profitable growth, strategically managing costs and intelligently navigating risk. Such challenges require more than identifying new possibilities and making tough choices.

Congruity Advisors strategy capabilities span corporate and business unit strategy, integrated strategy management, sales and marketing. Our operational capabilities reflect the unique issues facing clients business, organizations frameworks and infrastructure operations. These are joined with capabilities in performance management and business restructuring.  We are aligned with a strategy & operations service line to promote the development of deep functional skills & experiences, and to maximize our ability to tailor solutions to solve our clients' business issues. Our Strategy service line focuses within industry segment on the issues of growth, margin, strategic risk management, and planning and execution.
