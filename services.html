Customer Intimacy

Most businesses need to transform to a Customer Intimacy business model at some point in their business cycle. Often this transformation, which puts successful customer outcomes at the heart of the business model, is undertaken as a response to shifting market conditions, eroding competitive standing, or deteriorating financial results. This is particularly true when mature product-oriented companies find that their innovation curve is no longer providing a long period of price differentiation for new products or product extensions. These symptoms often prompt companies to undertake reactive and point-specific change management initiatives, such as new Sales Training to approach sales efforts in a different way, rather than broader programs that fundamentally change the way they do business.

Customer Intimacy is a business transformation initiative that entails a wide reaching, strategically driven campaign to drive brand differentiation, and improve the way in which a company conducts business. For those companies who are embracing this transformation, many have begun to realize that they must make the journey from a product-centric business model, to a solutions-oriented business model that enables differentiated and exceptional service experiences to happen. By offering customers a highly differentiated experience that also enhances operational efficiencies, companies can position themselves to thrive in a volatile business climate.

Despite strong agreement that customer intimacy is fundamental to success, many corporate executives say their organizations are only somewhat, or not at all prepared to successfully execute a customer intimacy model today. This transformation to a differentiated, customer-centric and solution-oriented business model is not simply a matter of changing one big idea. Instead, it requires changing many tightly integrated little ideas to create a holistic and strategic approach. Because of the broad, enterprise-wide nature of customer intimacy, companies also require seasoned leaders with insight and experience to navigate across all functions of the business.

Customer Experience Management

Customer experience is a powerful differentiator in today’s marketplace and has a profound impact on business success. 55% of consumers would pay more for a better experience, and, according to Forrester, the revenue impact from a 10% improvement in customer experience can translate to over $1 billion. To keep pace, companies must not only listen to and accurately interpret the voice of their customers, they must also proactively anticipate their needs to stand out from the noisy crowd.

Our approach to Customer Experience Management (CEM) takes a customer-centric perspective of the end-to-end journey that comprises the customer experience. We do this in the context of customer needs, expectations and experiences. Through strategic planning, applied technology solutions, and operational engagement, we can assist you in offering an experience to your customers that is predictable, repeatable, and strengthens your brand presence throughout the lifecycle of their journey.

Congruity Advisors believes that the transformation to a differentiated, customer-centric business is not a matter of changing one big idea, but rather changing a lot of little ideas, that are tightly integrated to create a holistic, enterprise-wide, approach.
